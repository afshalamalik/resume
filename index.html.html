How it works

Teachable Machine allows you to train your own model using your webcam, microphone, or uploaded data. No coding skills required!

Simply follow these steps:

  1. Choose the type of model you want to create (image, sound, or pose)
  2. Collect or upload your training data
  3. Train your model using our intuitive interface
  4. Export your model and use it in your own projects
